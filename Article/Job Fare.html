Oscar's Skill and Job Fare

What is the Leisure Centre actually for? It has the pool you can ogle with the floor to ceiling windows facing the street, I think that helped them decide where the over-60's class would be held, not that I'm complaining.

Soon it'll all be one big orgy, businesses and prospective workers from miles around dolling themselves up, hitting the town, and sizing up the freshest cuts of meat on the block. I wonder who's whoring themselves out more, us or them?

So I trimmed my beard, upstairs and downstairs, borrowed a pair of knee pads and got ready to do my due diligence as an up and coming journalistic intern.

I'll probably get people questioning the ethics of covering this while not wearing a press pass, well:-

  1. You need credentials to get a press pass, and if I had those I wouldn't be at a job fare
  2. I told most of them, that I both wanted to be a journalist and was explicitly there for journalism
  3. All of this information was publicly available, and normally that means it's in the bottom of a locked filing cabinet in a disused bathroom.
